About me

A twenty-late-something year old guy from sunny Malta, he is a dedicated gamer with a variety of gaming interests, which are mainly focused on PC, Mobile and a handful console games. Being brought up with a trusty Gameboy, he also enjoys a fair few handheld games on platforms such as; the Nintendo 3DS series and the Switch.

In terms of games, his taste varies and are mostly dependant on the game, such as the XCOM series, the Warcraft series and League of Legends are amongst his favorite. In addition to this, he enjoys playing a mixture of Jam games with a particular interest in innovative or games with fresh ideas.
